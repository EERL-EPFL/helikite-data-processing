.. py:function:: pressure_to_altitude(pressure: float, pressure_at_start: float, temperature_at_start: float, altitude_at_start: float = 0) -> float

   Convert pressure to altitude

   :param pressure: Pressure to convert to altitude
   :type pressure: float
   :param pressure_at_start: Pressure at start of flight
   :type pressure_at_start: float
   :param temp_at_start: Temperature at start of flight
   :type temp_at_start: float
   :param altitude_at_start: Altitude at start of flight (default 0)
   :type altitude_at_start: float

   :returns: **altitude** -- Altitude in meters
   :rtype: float

   .. rubric:: Example

   >>> pressure_to_altitude(101325, 101325, 20)
   0.0
   >>> pressure_to_altitude(101325, 101325, 20, 1000)
   1000.0
